    How to Play

    • Type your pilot name to log your scores and climb the leaderboard.
    • The raygun auto-sprays bubbly goo – just steer the paddle over the bugs.
    • Move the blaster with:
      • Arrow keys ⬅ ➡ or A / D on a keyboard
      • Tap on empty space to slide it there on a tablet
    • The spray pops any bug it touches and causes a splash burst for bonus hits.
    • Grab power-ups:
      • Freeze – pause new bugs.
      • 2x – double points.
      • Mega – blast all bugs on screen.
    • You have 30 seconds. Try to beat the best score!
